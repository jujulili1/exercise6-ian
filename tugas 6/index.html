3 Idiots

Directed by: Rajkumar Hirani

Produced by: Vidhu Vinod Chopra

Screenplay by: Abhijat Joshi

3 Idiots is a 2009 Indian Hindi-language coming-of-age comedy-drama film co-written (with Abhijat Joshi) and directed by Rajkumar Hirani. Starring Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, Sharman Joshi, Kareena Kapoor, Boman Irani and Omi Vaidya in the lead roles.
